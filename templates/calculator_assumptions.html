• Adult Escapement
      Default adult escapement values are the 2001-2015 Grandtab median escapement.
  • Habitat Acerage
      Default spawning and rearing values are the habitat acerage at the 1980-1999 CalSim median flows.
  • Territory
      Each spawning pair requires 12.4 square meters and each fry requires 0.054 square meters.
  • Egg to Fry
      The number of fry is a result of the number of spawning pairs supported by the available spawning habitat multiplied by the number of eggs per female and the egg to fry survival rate.

      sex ratio: 50/50
      number of eggs per female: 5,500
      egg to fry survival rate: 0.485
